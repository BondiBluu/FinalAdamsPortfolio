My Resume

Hi! I'm Elle!

I'm an art student that's deeply interested in creating art that doubles as an immersive experience. Although I love Character Design, Animation, and practically every kind of art imaginable, I've found a passion for the interactive world of games. There's just something magical about connecting a player with a world you've built especially for them, and watching them explore it with such involved fascination!

I have an endless fascination in philosophy, psychology, and the study of society through human nature and morality. These topics end up being the foundations of my games and storytelling, with an aim of either posing internal questions for the player, or opening a new possible way of looking at the world!

Currently, I’m working on a Tactical Teambuilder Gauntlet game project, a Fortune Teller game project, and an upcoming comic focused heavily on society with a strong influence of cultural mythology.

If you want to keep an eye on what I’m working on, follow my socials!

I look forward to sharing my work with you!
